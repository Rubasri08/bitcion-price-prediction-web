Frequently Asked Questions

Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that you can buy, sell, and exchange directly, without an intermediary like a bank.

You can buy Bitcoin from cryptocurrency exchanges or brokers, or directly from individuals through peer-to-peer platforms.

Bitcoin can be highly volatile and speculative, so it's important to research and consider your risk tolerance before investing.

Bitcoin mining involves using powerful computers to solve complex mathematical problems. Successful miners earn new Bitcoins as rewards.

Bitcoin transactions are irreversible, and the value is highly volatile. Make sure to understand these risks before using or investing in Bitcoin.
